# Final Commentary
## Why I chose LibreOffice For My Final Project
  The biggest factor in my choosing LibreOffice as the tool I would create my final project with was a simple one: it was super easy for me to learn the software's mechanics, layout, and formatting style. It was a skill I picked up quickly because it was a mix between the standard Google Docs and the Microsoft Word Software, two tools which I am well versed in as a technical writer major, as being adept in all genres and versions of popular writing software is a likely skill I will need in my future career.
  Regarding my future career, I chose LibreOffice because it felt most relevant to the specific subplan in the TWC major. As an aspiring Environmental Scientist Technical Writer, I felt that I would often be tasked with creating book interiors, white papers, and reports that were almost identical to my final project, giving me not only projects to add to my portfolio to show during interviews and job hunting. My ability to organize and make complex topics, research, or ideas into a well-formatted and cohesive layout that could allow almost anyone to understand, follow along, and digest easily is why I wanted to get the most practice and time with LibreOffice, and this final project allowed me to go back and refresh my memory on these newer skills, so I don't forget them.
